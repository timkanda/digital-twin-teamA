Avanade is the leading provider of innovative digital, cloud, and advisory services, industry solutions, and design-led experiences across the Microsoft ecosystem. Every day, our 60,000 professionals in 26 countries make a genuine human impact for our clients, their employees, and their customers.

## About the Role

Join our high performing Software Engineering team, where you'll develop cutting-edge solutions using Microsoft technologies. As a Junior Full-Stack Developer, you'll collaborate with developers, designers, and architects to deliver quality solutions for enterprise clients.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and maintain web applications using React, TypeScript, and .NET
- Collaborate with developers, designers, and architects to deliver quality solutions
- Participate in all phases of the software development lifecycle
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code
- Participate in code reviews and continuous improvement initiatives
- Work with Azure cloud services for deployment and hosting
- Support and maintain existing client applications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, or related field
