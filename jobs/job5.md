# Specialist Systems Software Engineer

## Company: BarBooks

**Source**: Indeed.com.au - January 2026

## Location: Melbourne VIC 3004 (Hybrid)

## Salary: $80,000 - $85,000 AUD + super

## About BarBooks

BarBooks is a leading Australian legal technology company providing practice management software to barristers and legal professionals. Our cloud-based platform helps legal practitioners manage their practices efficiently.

## About the Role

We're looking for a Specialist Systems Software Engineer to join our growing team. You'll be leading enterprise support initiatives while also contributing to development projects. This role offers a blend of technical support leadership and software development.

## Key Responsibilities

- Leading enterprise support division and mentoring junior developers
- Providing advice, guidance and expertise in developing proposals and strategies
- Troubleshooting and resolving complex technical issues
- Contributing to application development and improvements
- Working with customers to understand their requirements
- Documenting technical solutions and procedures
- Participating in release planning and deployment
- Supporting integration with third-party systems

## Essential Requirements

- 2+ years experience in software development or technical support
- Strong programming skills in at least one language (Python, JavaScript, C#)
- Experience with web application development
- Understanding of databases (SQL Server, PostgreSQL)
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
- Strong customer communication skills
- Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical users
- Experience mentoring or guiding junior team members
- Right to work in Australia

## Desirable Skills

- Experience with .NET or Python web frameworks
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (Azure, AWS)
- Understanding of legal technology or professional services
- Experience with SaaS products
- API development and integration experience
- ITIL or support methodology knowledge
