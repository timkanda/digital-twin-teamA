# Graduate Software Developer - Enterprise Solutions

## Company: CloudScale Solutions Pty Ltd

## Location: Brisbane, QLD (Hybrid - flexible arrangements)

## Salary: $65,000 - $80,000 AUD + super

## About CloudScale Solutions

CloudScale Solutions is a leading Australian technology consultancy delivering enterprise software solutions for government and private sector clients. We specialize in digital transformation, cloud migration, and custom software development.

## The Role

We're looking for a passionate Graduate Software Developer to join our growing team. This is an excellent opportunity for a recent graduate to gain hands-on experience across diverse projects while receiving structured mentorship and career development.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop web applications using modern frameworks (React, Angular, or Vue)
- Build backend services with Node.js, Python, or Java
- Collaborate with senior developers on client projects
- Write unit and integration tests
- Participate in Agile ceremonies (standups, sprints, retrospectives)
- Learn and apply best practices in software development
- Document technical solutions and user guides
- Support client deployments and troubleshooting

## Essential Requirements

- Recent graduate (within 2 years) with a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field
- Understanding of at least one programming language (JavaScript, Python, Java, or C#)
- Knowledge of web development fundamentals (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
- Familiarity with version control (Git)
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication
- Ability to work in a team environment
- Australian citizen or permanent resident (for government project clearances)

## Desirable Skills

- Experience with React, Angular, or Vue.js
- Knowledge of backend frameworks (Express, Django, Spring Boot)
- Understanding of databases (SQL and NoSQL)
- Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS, Azure)
- Personal projects or university capstone experience
- Internship experience in software development

## What We Offer

- Structured 12-month graduate program with mentorship
- Rotation across different project teams
- Formal training and certification opportunities (AWS, Azure, Agile)
- Clear career progression pathway
- Competitive salary with annual reviews
- Flexible work arrangements
- Active social committee and team events
- Modern Brisbane CBD office with end-of-trip facilities
